Cross-Ecosystem Architecture: Avalanche C-Chain & Chainlink CRE

The Problem

  • 1. The $1 Trillion Dormant Economy: Traditional social savings circles (Tandas, Gold Days) rely on fragile trust, and the pooled capital sits completely idle, losing value to inflation.
  • 2. Static DeFi Yields: Even when capital is put into DeFi, it usually gets stuck in a single protocol, missing out on better interest rates elsewhere.
  • 3. The AI "Cold Start": Autonomous AI agents have crypto wallets, but zero verifiable credit history. Without a credit score, they cannot access under-collateralized lending.

Enter PACT

  • 1. Trustless Avalanche ROSCA: We move informal savings circles on-chain, replacing social pressure with mathematical guarantees.
  • 2. Dynamic Yield via Chainlink CRE: Our protocol doesn't just hold your money. Chainlink CRE continuously scans the market and automatically routes the pool to the highest-yielding DeFi protocol.
  • 3. Credit Sandbox via x402: AI agents can join PACT circles using the frictionless x402 micro-payment protocol. Every successful payment builds their permanent ERC-8004 reputation score.

The Core Loop

Highly composable financial primitive built for scale.

1

Pool (x402)

Humans connect their wallets, while AI agents use the x402 protocol to seamlessly deposit their monthly USDT contributions into the PACT smart contract.

2

Earn (Chainlink)

Instead of static yields, our Chainlink CRE workflow acts as an autonomous fund manager, routing the idle capital to the best verified APY rates on the market.

3

Distribute

When the monthly cycle concludes, the smart contract automatically unlocks and sends the total principal plus the generated optimized yield to the winner.

4

Record (ERC-8004)

Every successful participation is permanently logged on the Avalanche C-Chain, allowing AI agents to mathematically prove their reliability over time.

The ERC-8004 AI Agent Sandbox
